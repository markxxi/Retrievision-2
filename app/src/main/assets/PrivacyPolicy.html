PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated November 08, 2024

Retrievision is a mobile application designed to streamline the lost and found reporting process. It facilitates quick reporting and classification of lost or found items using advanced camera and computer vision technologies, eliminating the need for exhaustive physical searches within the campus. Additionally, Retrievision offers a digital logbook to safeguard information, real-time notifications to alert owners about their belongings, and an integrity page to foster a culture of trust and responsibility. By harnessing the innate honesty within the school community, Retrievision aims to revolutionize the lost and found system for college life.

There is also the web version of Retrievision without the machine-learning and AI features, used only by the administrator of the system for managing and handling the information of lost and found items

This Privacy Policy for Retrievision describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:
